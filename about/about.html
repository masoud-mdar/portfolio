Since the 90s and “not so much fancy computers” era, I’ve always been fascinated by how someone can make computers do awesome things by saying the right words. Computer programming is the most realistic abracadabra that someone can find: “I will create as I speak” or I will create as I program!

My passion about computers and programming them started with saying Hello to the World in my oldest programming language friend, Basic. Sure, solving Project Euler problems with Basic is not as much fun as it seems to be, but programming’s inner charm brings us all the fun wee need! I am always learning and never giving up.
